Malcom-X: The Black Revolution Requires Bloodshed

The speech referenced here is a speech made by Malcolm-X, a popular marxist civil rights leader. Much of his ideologies are white washed, same with Martin Luther King Jr. Both of these men were socialist in some sorts, with Malcolm-X being marxist. One of the biggest differences between the 2 of them are their methods, while King advocated for non-violence, Malcolm-X called for a revolution. This is what this speech is discussing, the need for a violent revolution to change the system as a whole, not just the racist system but the capitalistic system.
